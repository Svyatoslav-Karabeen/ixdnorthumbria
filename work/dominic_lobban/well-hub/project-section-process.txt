<p>We looked at the current market, as well as exploring Well Pharmacy brand values. We considered tangible devices for home re-ordering, and even a futuristic smart home cabinet to track medication. We also thought about a diary based system for users to understand their health better, for a peace of mind. </p>
<p>With such a diverse user pool, we needed to create a flexible solution. We decided to create a system based on Big Data. We knew Well pharmacy promised ‘personal healthcare in the heart of the community.’ We therefore enforced the idea of community, promising health news specific to the individual, as well as their local community.</p>
